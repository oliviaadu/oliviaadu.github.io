Why is being GREEN important?

Environmental benefits:

- Protect ecosystems
- Improve air and water quality
- Conserve Resources
- Reduce emissions

Economic benefits:

- Energy and water savings
- Increase property value
- Reduce operating cost
- Improve productivity and satisfaction

Social benefits:

- Improve quality of life
- Enhance comfort and health
- Improve air, thermal, and acoustic environments

How do I improve my score?

If you wish to improve your “green” score, view our tips to maintain a more environmentally-friendly building.

Electricity

  • use “green energy”: geothermal, wind, hydroelectric, nuclear, solar
  • unplug/turn off unused electronics
  • use extension cords as they do not waste when electronics are not being used, switch off when not used
  • use power saver mode on computer
  • uses LED or CFL light bulbs

HVAC

  • get proper insulation
  • use tankless water heaters
  • use programmable thermostat
  • plant more trees on south and west sides as the leaves block sun in summer but not in winter
  • seal any air leaks
  • install multiple-pane windows as they provide insulation and reduce noise

Water

  • harvest rainwater for future use
  • install water conserving fixtures such as faucets and toilets
  • use tankless water heaters

Air

  • don’t travel unless you have to
  • work from home
  • plant more trees, especially on south and west sides since they block sun in the summer

Waste

  • Recycle! Paper, bottles, cans, e-waste...
  • Compost! Kitchen scraps, yard trimmings...
  • Donate! Clothes, electronics, furniture...

Resources

  • print less
  • go digital
  • use email instead of snail mail
  • print on both sides of the paper
  • reuse paper as scrap paper
